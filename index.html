Audio samples from "Integrating Scalable Attention Architecture with Multi-Resolution Full-Band Discriminator for High Fidelity Audio Waveform Generation"

Abstract: Generative Adversarial Network vocoders have gained significant attention due to their ability to generate high-quality audio with fast inference speeds. In this work, we propose a scalable hierarchical architecture incorporating multi-order spatial attention and channel attention stacking is developed to enhance feature representation learning, this design enables progressive refinement of acoustic features through attention-guided processing. Moreover, we propose a multi-resolution full-band discriminator framework that simultaneously capture local spectral details and global waveform coherence with multiple resolutions, effectively mitigating blurring artifacts that commonly occur in conventional discriminators, our discriminator explicitly integrates both the magnitude information and complex-domain representations by jointly processing the magnitude, real, and imaginary components.
